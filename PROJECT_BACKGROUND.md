# Occupational Therapy Capstone Project — Background

## Overview

This project is a website serving as a resource for an **occupational balance and wellness educational program** designed for **collegiate athletes**. It is part of an occupational therapy capstone research project.

## Purpose

The program aims to improve collegiate athletes':

- **Perceived occupational balance** — helping athletes balance the demands of sport, academics, and personal life
- **Health and wellness** — with a specific focus on mental health outcomes

## Educational Content Areas

The website delivers educational materials organized around four core topics:

1. **Time Management** — strategies for balancing athletics, academics, social life, and self-care
2. **Sleep Hygiene** — evidence-based practices for improving sleep quality and establishing healthy sleep routines
3. **Nutrition Habits** — guidance on fueling for performance, recovery, and overall well-being
4. **Stress Management / Mindfulness Strategies** — techniques for managing competitive and academic stress, building resilience, and incorporating mindfulness into daily routines

## Target Audience

Collegiate athletes who face unique occupational balance challenges due to the demands of competitive sport alongside academic responsibilities.

## Key Concepts

- **Occupational Balance** — the distribution of time and energy across meaningful activities (self-care, productivity, leisure) in a way that supports health and well-being
- **Wellness** — a holistic view of health encompassing physical, mental, emotional, and social dimensions
- **Occupational Therapy Lens** — the program is grounded in OT principles, viewing daily activities as "occupations" and addressing how their balance impacts overall health
