About This Program

The purpose of "An Occupational Balance and Wellness Program for Collegiate Athletes" is to promote the occupational balance, health, well-being, and mental health of collegiate athletes through educational intervention. The sessions focus on primary areas that can be impacted by the busy schedules of collegiate athletes. The topics included in this program are time management, sleep and rest, nutrition and hydration, and stress management/mindfulness.

Occupational Therapy

Aims to promote health and quality of life in all individuals through meaningful "occupations", also known as the things you do every day and what you enjoy.
