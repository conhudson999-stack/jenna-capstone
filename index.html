About This Program

This educational program is grounded in occupational therapy principles, viewing daily activities as meaningful "occupations" that shape overall health and well-being. Designed specifically for collegiate athletes, it addresses the unique challenges of balancing competitive sport, academics, and personal life. Through evidence-based strategies across four key wellness areas, the program aims to improve perceived occupational balance and support holistic health — including mental health — for student-athletes.
